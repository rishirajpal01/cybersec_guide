Why this was created?

With the aim of making it a one stop guide so users wont have to fumble on search engines to find tiny bits of information.

Our main aims to the project were:

  • 01 Information Integrity

    With syllabuses being updated daily for new and old certification to counter the ever growing world of cyber threats a lot of information on websites is obsolete and not even useful anymore, out website aims to keep it updated so the users will never have to worry about getting wrong or obsolete information.

  • 02 Ease of use

    A very begineer friendly but at the same time modern looking website we aim to get as many users on board as possible at the same time keep things minimalistic as to not get them overwhelmed with the ghosts of cyber world, but giving them enough resources to get them started in their journey.

  • 03 All level of information

    Even if you are a veteran in Cyber world there is never a time when you can rest and say ,"Yeah I have done everything". With almost as many as 20,000+ attacks a day there is always something to learn and our webpage aims to do that by keeping you engaged and knwoing about what is happening in the world of Cyber.
